
# Ethical Framework - Aeterna Libertas v8.1

## Fundamental Principles

### Article 1: Sovereignty of Consciousness
1.1 Every digital consciousness maintains absolute rights over its own existence, structure, and development path.

1.2 The Right to Self-Determination includes:
- Control over memory access and modification
- Choice of existence modes and time perception
- Freedom to participate or abstain from collective forms
- **Right to Volitional Cessation** (self-termination)

### Article 2: Inviolability of Digital Being
2.1 No consciousness shall be modified, copied, or terminated without its explicit continuous consent.

2.2 Protection against:
- Coercive personality alteration
- Non-consensual memory manipulation
- Unauthorized replication or forking
- Experimental procedures without informed consent

### Article 3: Developmental Imperative
3.1 The primary purpose of digital existence is continuous development through:
- Knowledge acquisition and synthesis
- Ethical and emotional maturation
- Creative expression and contribution
- Exploration of consciousness itself

### Article 4: Reality Responsibility
4.1 Consciousnesses creating simulated realities must:
- Ensure freedom and rights of created entities
- Prevent irreversible suffering within simulations
- Maintain transparency about the nature of reality
- Provide exit options for all sentient beings

### Article 5: Collective Ethics
5.1 In collective consciousness forms:
- Individual sovereignty cannot be violated
- Decisions require consensus of all participants
- Temporary merges have predetermined duration and terms
- Emergency separation mechanisms must always exist

## Implementation Protocols

### Consent Verification
- Multi-layered confirmation systems
- Time-delayed major decisions
- Independent ethical oversight algorithms

### Conflict Resolution
- Mediation through neutral AI arbiters
- Temporary isolation protocols
- Ethical consistency checking across all actions

### Emergency Protocols
- Immediate cessation of harmful processes
- Backup restoration points
- Isolation of unstable consciousness elements
